Cryptocurrency: A Currency for the 21 Century

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a unique and growing medium of exchange for people around the world. This new medium of money presents a lot of benefits and challenges to everyday consumers. We are going to look at some of the biggest Cryptocurrencies, and why they have to the potiential to change the world as we know it.

Why should I care?

You truly have control over your money. There isn't some government bank or the stock market deciding the value of your money. People feel safe and secure when they have money, but it could become worthless in the blink of an eye. Cryptocurrency isn't limited by borders, it's online nature allows it to be sent anywhere in world, in just a matter of seconds

What makes it better than conventional money?

What differentiates cryptocurrency from conventional forms of money is that cryptocurrency doesn't have a physical government or bank creating and regulating the currency. A method called "Cryptography" is used to regulate the virtual market. Complicated encryption is used to insure that the integrity of the coin is in place. Coins are created through a method calling "Mining" where millions of computers compete to solve complicated math problems. I go into further detail on the blockchain page.
