A Short Explanation of the Blockchain

The incredible technology behind Bitcoin is truly revolutionary, and has a far reaching impact for the entire world. At its core, the blockchain logs every transaction that has ever happened over the Bitcoin network. Essentially, it's a permanent ledger for Bitcoin. Transactions are combined into what is called a block, this block is then found by the process called mining, which I have discussed before. The redundancy of the Bitcoin network insures people can't cheat and the ledger is maintained by anyone who mines Bitcoin.
