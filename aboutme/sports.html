Many Sports, Many Opportunities


Sports have been a passion of mine, my whole life. From a young age, I took to basketball. It helped that I lived in Portland, home to the Portland Trailblazers and we saw games fairly often. I played on numerous basketball teams throughout my childhood. My favorite position was Low Post, closer the hoop. My height was an advantage and allowed me to catch rebounds and put it back up.


I picked up baseball around the age of 9 and from the beginning I was a natural. Throughout my years playing baseball, I played just about every position; wherever I was needed. I was a skilled hitter and fielder. My baseball years got cut short because the league I played for didn't end up getting enough players to field a proper team. This was a disappointment to say the least.


    My favorite Sports

  1. Baseball
  2. Football
  3. Basketball
  4. Golf
