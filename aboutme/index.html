About Hannes Widrig

 
 

My life

I was born on the West coast, in Portland, Oregon. I don't remember living in Oregon because I was much too young when my family moved to Orlando, FL. I loved Florida, but it was short-lived and unfortunately, circumstances prevented us from staying very long, and we moved a year later. Rochester, NY was an enormous transition from the sweltering, sunny summers of Florida. Today, I reside in Bergen, NY and am a full time student at Genesee Community College. I currently have a job at my college, part-time math tutoring in the Math Learning Lab. This fall, I'm transferring to Taylor University in Central Indiana. My major is Computer Science.

My family


My family is comprised of my mom, dad, and two younger siblings. They are quite rambunctious sometimes, but I love them. They've always been really supportive of everything I've done. My extended family doesn't happen to live anywhere near me. In face, with the exception of an uncle, they all live in California and Oregon. My grandparents also live on the west coast. I love spending time with family and friends, playing games, and photography.
