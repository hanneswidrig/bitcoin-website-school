Where I See Myself In 5 Years

 
 

I really want to move to the Midwest, preferably down somewhere like Dallas, Texas. Five years from now I'll have graduated from Taylor University and have a highly skilled job. I don't like to be boxed into a situation that inhibits my creativity, conceptual research and design from new technologies is where I want to find my place in this fast changing world.


Five years from now, I would like to married. I know that's not as common in today's society. At the end of the day, all you have are the people close to you. I'm just waiting for the right person to come along at this point.


I would love to travel across the world, below I've listed the top places I'd like to go. I don't know if I will get the opportunity to travel to even one of these places, but I hope to have the opportunity.
